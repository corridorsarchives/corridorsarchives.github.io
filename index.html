Corridor Archives

Welcome to Corridor Archives.

If you aren't careful, and you step into the wrong areas, you'll end up into the Backrooms; where it's nothing but the endless halls of areas that you think look familiar. Each hall looks the same, and has the same distinct smell of moist, dank carpet throughout the entirety of the halls. God save you if you hear something wandering around nearby, as it sure as hell has heard you. Corridor Archives is a Backrooms Wikipedia project made by only about 3 people. If you stumble across this and would like to support, consider talking about it with friends and sharing this website with people.
